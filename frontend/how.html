Current Architecture

How HalluGuard-Med Works

Each assistant response moves through generation, retrieval, verification, hallucination checks, and final safety reporting.

01

User Query/Image Upload

The user submits a medical question and may attach an image for visual context.

02

MedGemma Analysis

MedGemma produces the initial medical answer from the submitted input.

03

Medical Knowledge Retrieval

Qdrant Cloud RAG retrieves relevant medical evidence for verification.

04

Evidence Verification

Claims are checked against retrieved sources, citations, and support scores.

05

Hallucination Detection

Unsupported claims, contradictions, and source conflicts are flagged.

06

Confidence & Risk Scoring

The safety layer assigns confidence, risk level, and review guidance.

07

Final Safe Medical Report

The interface presents the answer with findings, evidence, risks, and recommendations.
